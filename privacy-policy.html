Privacy Policy

Shapes and Colors

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game "Shapes and Colors" ("the App", "we", "our") handles information. We are committed to protecting your privacy. The App is designed to be a simple arcade game and does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information.

Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account registration or login is required.
  • No name, email address, or other personal information is requested.
  • No usage analytics or tracking are implemented by the App.
  • High scores and game settings are stored locally on your device only and are never sent to any server.

Third-Party Services — Google AdMob

The App displays interstitial advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General device information (device model, operating system version)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data collection is governed by Google's own privacy policy. For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services .

Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the App does not collect any personal data itself, no special measures for children's data are necessary on our part. AdMob ad serving may be configured to comply with applicable regulations.

Data Storage

All game data (high scores and settings) is stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers by the App. Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
